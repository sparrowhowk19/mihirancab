About Us

Sri Lanka's Most Trusted Tourist Cab Service

Founded with a passion for sharing the beauty of Sri Lanka, Mihiran Cabs has been the first choice for international tourists seeking reliable, comfortable, and authentic island travel experiences.

Our fleet of air-conditioned vehicles, professionally trained drivers, and island-wide network means you can explore every corner of this paradise island — from the misty highlands of Ella to the golden beaches of Mirissa — with total peace of mind.
